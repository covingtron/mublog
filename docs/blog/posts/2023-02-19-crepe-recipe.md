# Blender Crepe Recipe

## Ingredients

- 500 mL (2 cups) milk
- 15–20 mL (1 tbsp–4 tsp) peanut oil or butter (butter for nut-free)
- 6 eggs
- 500 mL (2 cups) flour
- pinch of salt (1.25 mL / ¼ tsp), optional

## Directions

1. Blend all ingredients until smooth.
2. Grease pan with coconut oil or butter. Heat on medium-high. Put a towel under
   the pitcher to catch drips.
3. Pour mix to fill about 2/3 of the pan's radius. Tilt the pan in a circular
   motion to spread to the full radius — thinner than filling the whole pan at once.
4. Fry until bubbled and dry, then flip and cook until light brown on the bottom.
   Thin spatulas are much easier than thick ones.
5. Remove crepe, grease pan again, and repeat.
